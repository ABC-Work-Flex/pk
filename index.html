Meet Chef Tony

With roots deep in Southern soul food and Caribbean spice, Chef Tony has spent years crafting a menu that celebrates culture, flavor, and fire. His smoked meats, spiced rubs, and comfort classics are the heart of every Pirate’s Kiss event.

Whether it’s a backyard BBQ or a private in-home dinner, Chef Tony brings bold flavor and unforgettable presence to every gathering.

Proteins

Smoked Jerk Chicken

Our secret jerk blend is house-ground—featuring allspice, scotch bonnet, thyme, and garlic. Marinated for 24 hours, then slow-smoked over hickory—juicy, tender, and bold with Caribbean heat. (Gluten-free / can be made dairy-free)

Honey-Lacquered Baby Back Ribs

Fall-off-the-bone ribs glazed in our signature honey-smoke sauce (local honey, black pepper, cayenne, and Chef’s smoky chili paste). Slow-cooked for 6 hours for perfect tenderness. (Can be made spicy-mild upon request)

Andouille Sausage & Crab Gumbo

A rich brown roux base loaded with house-made Andouille (spiced by Chef Tony), fresh Gulf crab, shrimp, okra, and Creole trinity veggies. Simmered low and slow with in-house seasoned stock. (Gluten-free; contains shellfish)

Sides & Veggies

Mac & Cheese with Cajun Crust

Creamy four-cheese elbow pasta (sharp cheddar, Monterey Jack, Parmesan, smoked Gouda) baked under a crunchy, spiced cornbread crumble—hot, bubbly, and rich. (Vegetarian; can be made gluten-free with GF pasta)

Garlic Collard Greens

Fresh collards gently braised in house-rendered ham hocks (or smoked turkey for a veggie option), with roasted garlic, onion, and a dash of apple cider vinegar. (Vegetarian option available)

Red Beans & Rice

A New Orleans classic—slow-simmered red beans with smoked ham hocks, onion, celery, bell pepper, and Chef’s Cajun spice blend, served over steamed jasmine rice. (Gluten-free)

Breads & Extras

Buttermilk Cornbread

Handmade daily with stone-ground cornmeal, cultured buttermilk, and a hint of honey. Baked golden, served warm with herb-infused whipped butter (all in-house made). (Vegetarian)

Fried Plantains

Ripe Caribbean plantains, thin-sliced, fried until caramelized, and tossed in a light brown-sugar glaze. The perfect sweet side to spicy mains. (Vegan / gluten-free)

Desserts & Condiments

Peach Cobbler with Bourbon Glaze

Southern-style cobbler using local farm peaches, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a buttery biscuit topping. Drizzled with a house-made bourbon caramel (alcohol cooked off). (Vegetarian; contains dairy)

House-Made Hot Sauce Trio

Three unique blends—Caribbean Scotch Bonnet & Pineapple, Smoky Chipotle & Honey, and Fiery Habanero & Garlic. All crafted in-house with organic peppers, vinegar, and secret spice blends. (Vegan / gluten-free)
